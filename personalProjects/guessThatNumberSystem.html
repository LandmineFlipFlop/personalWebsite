Guess That Number System!

How It Works:

You get to see a list with the decimal notation next to it, and then have to guess what the rule for the numbers is.

  • All systems have a guaranteed notation for every positive integer to infinity
  • No number has more than 1 way one way to write it
  • Systems do not need to use Arabic numerals
